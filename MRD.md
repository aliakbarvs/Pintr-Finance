# Fintr - Market Requirements Document (MRD)

## 1. Market Opportunity

### Problem Statement
Indonesian workers (freelancers, contractors, office employees) struggle to track expenses because:
1. Manual entry is tedious → leads to incomplete records
2. No local-language expense apps → existing tools are too complex
3. Receipts pile up → can't remember what was spent

### Target Market
| Segment | Description | Size |
|---------|-------------|------|
| Primary | Freelancers & Gig workers | ~70M Indonesia |
| Secondary | Office employees | ~20M |
| Tertiary | Small business owners | ~5M |

### Pain Points
- Don't know where money went by end of month
- Receipts lost → can't claim expenses
- Monthly reports too late to be useful

---

## 2. Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|----------|----------|
| Money Manager | Feature-rich | Not Indonesian-focused |
| Wallet by BWA | Local | No OCR |
| Cashflow | Simple | No mobile app |

**Differentiation:** Receipt OCR + Local payment methods + Weekly insights

---

## 3. Value Proposition

### For Users
1. **Snap & Track** - Upload receipt → auto-categorize
2. **Local First** - GoPay, OVO, bank transfer support
3. **Weekly Insights** - Know your spending weekly, not monthly

### For Business (Future)
- Employee expense claims
- API for accounting integration
- Multi-user collaboration

---

## 4. Market Validation

### Survey Results (Internal)
- 10/10 would use receipt scanning
- 8/10 want weekly insights
- 7/10 prefer Indonesian-language app

### Competitor Downloads
- Money Manager Indonesia: 1M+ downloads
- Wallet by BWA: 500K+ downloads

---

## 5. Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: MVP Launch
- **Channel:** Instagram, TikTok (demo videos)
- **Incentive:** Free Pro tier for first 100 users
- **KPI:** 50 signups in 30 days

### Phase 2: Growth
- **Channel:** Influencer partnerships
- **Feature:** Referral program
- **KPI:** 500 users, 10% conversion

### Phase 3: Scale
- **Channel:** B2B sales to companies
- **Feature:** Business tier with team features
- **KPI:** 50 B2B clients

## 6. Risk Assessment
