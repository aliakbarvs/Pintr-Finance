## ❌ Challenges

### Pre-Development
1. **Receipt OCR is hard** - Need reliable API (Google Cloud Vision, AWS Textract)
2. **Indonesian banking APIs** - Limited open banking
3. **Mobile-first required** - Desktop-first won't work for target market

---

## 📚 Key Learnings

### From Other Projects
1. **Receipt scanning is the killer feature** - Everyone hates manual entry
2. **Local payment methods critical** - GoPay, OVO, bank transfers
3. **Weekly insights beat monthly** - Contractors need faster feedback

### Product Insights
- Target: Office workers + freelancers + contractors
- Pain: Don't know where money went
- Solution: Auto-categorize + weekly spending insights
